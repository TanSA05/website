I am a third-year Computer Science PhD student at the University of Toronto, advised by Robert Soden and Steve Easterbrook. My primary research area is Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), and my dissertation work conceptualizes and characterizes a human-centered approach to climate data and modeling that expands contemporary climate technologies to be more participatory, place-based and environmentally just. Keywords you can associate with my work include data practices, critical computing, climate change, and participatory design.
